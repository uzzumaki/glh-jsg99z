What is the World Baseball Classic and Mexico's Role?

The World Baseball Classic (WBC) is an international baseball tournament organized by Major League Baseball (MLB) and the Major League Baseball Players Association (MLBPA), featuring national teams from around the world, with Mexico being a consistent participant since its inception. These national teams, composed of professional players, compete for the title of world champion, drawing significant global viewership; for instance, the 2023 WBC recorded a total viewership of 132.8 million across all platforms, according to WorldBaseballClassic.com.

The WBC serves as a global stage for baseball, distinct from Olympic baseball. It gathers elite players from professional leagues, including MLB, NPB (Nippon Professional Baseball), and KBO (Korea Baseball Organization). Mexico's national team, a hypernym for its roster of players, has participated in all five editions of the tournament.
